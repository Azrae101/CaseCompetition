About the Game

Embark on an exciting journey to become a cybersecurity expert while helping Vestas secure its windmill operations. In this interactive game, you'll face quizzes, tasks, and challenges designed to enhance your cybersecurity knowledge.

Earn valuable points for every quiz and task you complete. Use your points to unlock exclusive rewards in our in-game point shop!
